
**Headline:**  
Build your plan in under 30 seconds

**Subheadline:**  
Answer a few quick questions so Revibe can create the right workout for you.

---

## 1. What is your main goal?

**Question text:**  
What do you want most right now?

**Answer choices:**
- Stay fit and maintain my physique
- Build muscle
- Lose fat
- Move better and feel less stiff

---

## 2. How often can you realistically work out?

**Question text:**  
How many days per week can you commit to?

**Answer choices:**
- 2 days
- 3 days
- 4 days
- 5+ days

---

## 3. How much time do you usually have?

**Question text:**  
How long should most workouts be?

**Answer choices:**
- 10–15 min
- 20–30 min
- 30–45 min
- 45+ min

---

## 4. What equipment do you have access to?

**Question text:**  
Where will you usually work out?

**Answer choices:**
- Full gym
- Dumbbells only
- Home, no equipment
- Bands / light equipment

---

## 5. Any pain or injuries we should work around?

**Question text:**  
Any body areas you want us to be careful with?

**Answer choices:**
- No pain or injuries
- Shoulder
- Back
- Knee
- Other

**If user taps `Other`, open a small input:**  
Tell us briefly

## Best UI behavior

- One vertically stacked card layout
- Each question shown as a compact section
- Tap-to-select answers
- Progress should feel lightweight, not heavy
- No long forms or typing unless `Other` is selected

---

## Recommended product behavior after submit

Once the user taps **Create My Plan**, the app should immediately generate:

- Recommended weekly structure  
  Example: `3-day full body`
- Today’s first workout  
  Example: `18-min Upper Body Starter`
- Short explanation  
  Example: `Built for your goal, time, and equipment`

## Product note

Use only these five questions on the first screen.  
Do not ask fitness level yet unless you really need it.

Instead, infer it from:
- First workout completion
- Movement quality
- Pace
- Whether the user struggles with volume
